Tudor in the Pines

Tudor in the Pines is a spectacular estate in the Philippines comprised of seven (7) individual mansions within a guarded property, hosting a maximum of 30 guests. It is hidden within the lush greenery of Baguio City's pinewood forest. With the ease of many road entrances to and from the city, as well as to other Cordillera highland regions. Tudor in the Pines is an ideal home base for exploring the Philippines' northern marvels.
